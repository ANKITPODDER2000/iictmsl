ON CAMPUS ROUND

  • Atleast 30 teams should participate from the college. Each team must have a minimum of 3 members and maximum of 4 members.
  • These 30 teams would be divided equally in 3 different rooms. There would be three judges in each of these rooms.
  • This round will be held in the online mode. Top 3 teams from each room would go for the final round in the On Campus Program.
  • In the final round - top 2 - 3 teams will be selected based on the number of participations.
  • The winners will participate in the Regionals 2021 Summit.
  • The problem statement for Hult Prize 2021 would be released in mid of August. The tentative date for the OnCampus round is 20th September, 2020.
  • 2 days Bootcamp to get the students acquainted with the On Campus Round.
  • This would also include brain-storming sessions on the given problem statement.

REGIONALS 2021 SUMMIT

  • The Regionals is proposed to be conducted in February, 2021.
  • This round is organized in around 121 cities around the world. In India, it is organized only in Mumbai, Maharashtra.
  • The top 40 teams would get a chance to participate in the Global Accelerator Program.
  • Each of the 3 top teams will be awarded 10,000 $.

GLOBAL ACCELERATOR PROGRAM

  • The top 40+ teams will go through a rigorous curriculum.
  • Will transform their ideas into startups for a chance to win $ 1 Million.

CONCLUSION
